AIBotics @ Building Construction Technology (BCT) Expo 2023

Highlights

Building Construction Technology Expo (BCT Expo 2023) - Southeast Asian exhibition and conference platform focused on the building and construction industry. BCT Expo 2023 showcased a comprehensive range of building and construction technology, digital, machinery system and equipment by over 150 exhibitors and brands to over 4,000 trade visitors from all sectors of the building and construction industry and market. In line with the impacts of digital transformation trends, BCT Expo 2023 was also organized with focus on digital transformation of the building and construction industry. Under the theme “Towards Digitalization of the Building & Construction Industry - Are You Ready?”, this event helped drive the digital acceleration changes in every aspect of architectural design, building and construction through the application of digital technologies.
