AIBotics @ RobotWorld 2023

Highlights

RobotWorld is one of the three largest international robot exhibitions, consisting of an exhibition, competition and conference in one event. Since 2006, the Korean government decided to boost the industry as a whole, which was at an early stage of development.As a global trade fair for robot companies at home and abroad, ROBOTWORLD has been a reliable base for business growth and expanding demand for robots.
