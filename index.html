Connecting Technology to Human Values

—from Learning Tech to Ageing Tech

Technologies are certainly and increasingly outpacing our ability to deal with them as humans. The fundamental problem is that we are not well trained to be systematic thinkers naturally (given a choice the larger human community would choose to follow instructions.) Skills of the future is not about attending another workshop or 6-month course, humans will have to be able to establish critical and rational understanding why a situation happens, and what we want to do about, and then how and if we're not trained to be systematic thinkers, technology will outpace us. Young or old, AIBotics shall have a message for you; join us at our next AIBotics meet-up as we journey on to leverage technology to make us a Better Human! See you at AIBotics soon!

Why AIBotics?

AIBotics brings industry leading solutions to a global platform dedicated to demonstrating how advanced technologies can be leveraged to augment human capability and potential.

This understanding and implementation of AI & Robotics across the wide range of enterprises aim to bring together the most progressive end-users, first-class speakers, and innovative solution providers.

AIBotics 2023 will be a 2-day convention and exhibition to be held at the Singapore Expo. It carries the theme of “Connecting Technology to Human Values” — from Learning Tech to Ageing Tech.
