Collaborating with Partners to Engage AI in Robotics

AIBotics @ Partner Events 2025

AIBotics will be co-hosting a number of satellite forums with our Partners from cities around the world.
These forums will address specific technology issues in the local context. All are welcome to attend these events.

The Exhibition

AI & Robotic technologies have made our lives easier in multiple ways. We can access multiple benefits right through our smartphones with the power of digital assistants like Google Assistant, Siri, Alexa, as well as smart machines andsocial robots. In today’s world, multiple industries such as healthcare, e-commerce, financial services, etc., are leveraging the benefits of AI to the fullest of its potential. The technology has helped businesses grow in leaps and bounds with improved quality, security, and efficiency.

Due to the complex challenges that the various industry face, the growth in the industryis severely limited. Since 2023, AIBotics as a conference has been brought closer to the local audiences. Together with our local partners, AIBotics has been collocated in Singapore, Thailand, Philippines, China and Korea. We believe that in this approach, we can bring an exchange of expertise closer to the local audience and motivate greater thought innovation and address more comprehensive issues locally.
