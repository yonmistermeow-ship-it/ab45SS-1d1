AIBotics @ BuildTech Asia 2023

Highlights

The BuildTech Asia 2023 edition was a success, with over 80 exhibitors from 15 regions, and had more than 4,000 visitors over the 3-day event. With over 200 facilitated business meetings, BTA served as a strategic platform for meaningful connections to take place between both exhibitors and delegates alike, effectively accelerating the adoption of digitalised solutions and smart technologies in the key segments of building materials, construction machineries, architectural finishes, facilities management, and productive technology.
