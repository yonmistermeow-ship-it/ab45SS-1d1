The Exhibition

AIBotics 2023 will drive and showcase AI and Robotic applications across a variety of sectors, including technologies that augment learning capabilities and extend human potential—with a focus on enabling active ageing and healthy living.

The Conference will explore development challenges and offer recommendations for optimising these technologies while safeguarding essential human values. This unique gathering transforms visionary ideas into real-world, ethically guided applications.

Attendees will engage with responsible solutions transforming the way we live, work, and play—while the next-gen workforce and academia gain insights into the future of tech adoption.

If you have an innovative technology to share, seize the opportunity to exhibit it at AIBotics 2023 and connect with a dynamic, forward-thinking audience!
