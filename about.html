AI in Robotics

Leveraging AI in Robotics (AIBotics) — a proposition to empower and unlock human potential through intelligent robotics: where AI and innovation unite to unlock new possibilities in teamwork and technology.

By integrating AI with Robotics, we can amplify human creativity and problem-solving. This powerful collaboration not only enhances efficiency and precision but also opens new frontiers in innovation, transforming how we work, learn, and grow together in an increasingly intelligent world.

International Recognition

Program a holistic view of how humans can benefit from connecting technology to human values. We need better humans to leverage advanced technologies.

Conceived in 2018, AIBotics serves as a platform on the global stage to address the adoption of Augmented Intelligence in robotic technology. It reviews the innovative and responsible use of Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, and relevant technologies aimed at creating a better living environment.

Endorsed and supported by the International Alliance of Robotics Associations (IARA) and the Global Robot Cluster (GRC), this event brings together renowned experts to discuss and showcase proven technologies and projects from around the world.
