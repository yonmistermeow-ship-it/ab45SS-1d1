AIBotics @ Robot World (RW) 2025

Highlights

Robot World (Robot World 2025) - Robot World Korea 2025 (Robot World Korea 2025) is a major international robotics exhibition held at KINTEX, bringing together companies, researchers, and innovators to showcase the latest advancements in robotics and AI. The event features industrial automation systems, service robots, and emerging technologies, alongside conferences, competitions, and networking opportunities. It serves as a key platform for exploring how robotics is shaping industries and everyday life, highlighting South Korea's leading role in the global robotics ecosystem.
